Greentrip is a study examining how sustainable suggestions affect travel behavior.
Users will receive suggestions on their latest trips as well as the option to see suggestions for all their trips.
The suggestions are generated based on restaurant location. For example if the user is driving to a coffee shop 5 miles away but we see there is one of equal rating .5 miles away, the user will be suggested to go to that one instead.
Users will also be given surveys regarding their transportation patterns and opinions on the suggestions
The experiment will run for approximately 2 months and there will be compensation for all participants
