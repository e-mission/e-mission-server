Welcome to E-mission!

e-mission is a tool to instrument the end-to-end multi-modal travel experience, from the personal to the societal scale. It automatically

generates a personal travel profile collects user reported perceptual data makes the aggregate heatmaps and metrics available for public use and includes an integration with a game for motivation.


The links below are for installing and configuring the phone app, and are intended to be used on the phone. If you have opened this message on your desktop, please forward the message to your phone and click them there. Alternatively, you can use the QR codes below each step with a QR code reader on your phone.
