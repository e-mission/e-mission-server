About the App

Why can't I choose my mode of transportation?

We decided to disable users from selecting their own mode of transportation in order to avoid dishonesty. We are currently inferring everyone's modes based on the same criteria.

How is the leaderboard determined?

The leaderboard is determined by everyone's normalized carbon footprint over the past seven days and is updated on a daily basis.

How is my bear's mood and size determined?

Your polar bear's mood reflects your carbon emissions in the past day. The happier it is, the less carbon you emitted (compared to last week). Every day your bear remains happy, it will slowly grow larger. Once your bear becomes sad, it will reset to its original size.
